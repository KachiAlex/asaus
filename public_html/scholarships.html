ASA-USA Scholarship Program

The ASA-USA Scholarship Program is committed to supporting students of Anambra descent pursuing higher education in the United States. Since inception, we have awarded over 200 scholarships totaling more than $500,000 to deserving students.

🎓

Undergraduate Scholarship

Up to $3,000

For students enrolled in an accredited 4-year university or college in the United States.

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Anambra State descent
  • Full-time enrollment
  • Demonstrated financial need
Apply Now

Graduate Excellence Award

Up to $5,000

For graduate and professional degree students demonstrating academic excellence and community leadership.

  • Minimum GPA of 3.5
  • Graduate program enrollment
  • Community service record
  • Two recommendation letters
Apply Now
📚

Community College Award

Up to $1,500

Supporting students at community colleges and vocational programs pursuing skills and knowledge.

  • Minimum GPA of 2.8
  • Community college enrollment
  • Essay submission required
  • ASA-USA member family

Timeline

Application Timeline

January – March

Applications open. Submit your completed application via email or the online portal.

April

Application review by the Scholarship Committee. Shortlisted candidates are notified.

May

Interviews conducted with shortlisted candidates.

June – July

Award announcements made. Recipients are honored at the National Convention.
