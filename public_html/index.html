About ASA-USA

Proud of Our Roots.
Committed to Our People.

Anambra State Association USA (ASA-USA) is the umbrella organization representing Anambra indigenes living in the United States.

Founded to foster unity, preserve our cultural identity, and create meaningful impact, ASA-USA serves as a bridge connecting our people across generations and borders.

Through scholarships, community outreach, youth empowerment, cultural preservation, healthcare initiatives, and strategic partnerships, we remain committed to strengthening the bonds of Ndi Anambra in America while giving back to our homeland.
